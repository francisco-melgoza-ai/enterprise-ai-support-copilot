# Account Access Procedure

Use this approved procedure when a customer cannot sign in, has a locked
account, or reports a failed password reset.

1. Confirm the customer can reach the sign-in page and is using the expected
   email address.
2. Ask the customer to complete the self-service password reset flow.
3. If multi-factor authentication is unavailable, direct the customer to the
   account recovery workflow.
4. Escalate to account security only when the customer reports suspected account
   takeover, repeated lockouts after reset, or missing recovery options.

Do not ask for passwords, full payment details, one-time passcodes, or security
answers in a support response.
