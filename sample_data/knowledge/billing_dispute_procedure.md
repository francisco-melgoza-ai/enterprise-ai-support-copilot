# Billing Dispute Procedure

Use this approved procedure when a customer disputes an invoice, payment,
refund, subscription charge, or billing adjustment.

1. Acknowledge the billing concern and ask for the invoice number or transaction
   reference.
2. Confirm the product, billing period, and account workspace involved.
3. Explain that support will review the charge before promising a refund,
   credit, or cancellation.
4. Escalate to billing operations when the customer reports duplicate charges,
   suspected fraud, failed refunds, or an urgent account-impacting billing hold.

Do not promise refunds, credits, tax changes, or legal conclusions unless the
approved billing team has confirmed them.
